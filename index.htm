🏢 About Etihad Airways

Etihad Airways is the national airline of the United Arab Emirates, based in Abu Dhabi 🏙️. Founded in 2003, it has quickly grown to become one of the world’s leading and most innovative airlines ✨.

Our mission is to deliver a superior flying experience, with world-class comfort, exceptional customer service, and a firm commitment to sustainability 🌱.

With a global network of destinations, Etihad connects people, cultures, and economies across the world 🌐.
